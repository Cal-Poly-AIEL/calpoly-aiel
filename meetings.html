Current Projects

Report on the State of Faculty Attitudes and Policy Around AI in Cal Poly Courses.

The lab is currently working in collaboration with the Academic Senate Ad Hoc Committee on Generative Artificial Intelligence to develop a report on the state of faculty attitudes and course policies around AI. The lab is taking a qualitative approach to this research, conducting faculty interviews across the six colleges to learn about the variety of perspectives and approaches concerning AI in Cal Poly courses. This data will also be used in tandem with student conversations to develop guidelines for building AI use policies and principles for student use. The report is planned to be released in June 2026.
