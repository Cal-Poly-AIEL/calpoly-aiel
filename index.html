Welcome

The Cal Poly Artificial Intelligence Ethics Lab (AI/EL) is an interdisciplinary student and faculty partnership designed to investigate the distinctive ethical, legal, and social challenges presented by emerging AI technologies. The lab is an opportunity for students to engage in conversations focused on addressing these challenges and the technical realities of any proposed solutions. Cal Poly AI/EL also provides an opportunity for students to learn about research in this field and connect with faculty about future endeavors.
